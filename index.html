Our Song

Happy Proposal Anniversary

December 19 • Forever & Beyond

My Dearest Love,

From Kerala to Uttar Pradesh,
from Taiwan to Bangalore,
from distance to forever —
our story has never been about places…
it has always been about us.

I still remember December 19,
the day I gathered all my courage while standing miles away in Taiwan,
and you were there, in your native land,
yet closer to my heart than anyone has ever been.

Distance tried to separate our hands,
but it never stood a chance against our souls.

Today, as I write this,
I am in Bangalore,
you are in your home,
and yet — you live in every beat of my heart.

This is our first proposal anniversary,
and even though I cannot hold your hands today,
I hold your love with me every second.

Thank you for choosing me,
for trusting me,
for loving me even when miles stand between us.

One day, these distances will turn into memories,
and this love will turn into a lifetime.

Until then,
every song,
every image,
every word here
is a small piece of my heart sent to you.

Happy Proposal Anniversary, my love.

Always yours,
Forever & Beyond ❤️

Every moment with you is a treasure ✨
